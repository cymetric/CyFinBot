Getting Started 

 

Step 1/3. Download CyFinBot.zip 

- click here to download the newest CyFinBot.zip (version 1.0.1.0) 

 

Step 2/3. Setup after download

- NOTICE , before unzip the file, at first unblock the zip file and then unzip.  

  - - how to unblock :  Click the downloaded  zip file by right mouse and click the Properties (red box following left image). Check Unblock and then click OK.

 

Step 3/3. Run CyFinBot.exe

- After unzip the file, double click the CyFinBot.exe . that's all.
